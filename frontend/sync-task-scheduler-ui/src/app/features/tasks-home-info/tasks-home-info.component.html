Tasks

The **Tasks** section allows you to manage and track your tasks in a simple, organized way. Whether it's personal goals, work-related duties, or group projects, Tasks makes it easy to keep everything in check.

With this feature, you can add new tasks, assign deadlines, and mark them as complete once finished. It's designed to help you focus on what matters and boost productivity.

Key features include:

  • Task Management: Easily create, update, and delete tasks.
  • Priority Levels: Assign priorities to tasks for better organization.
  • Status Tracking: Keep track of task progress and completion.

Stay productive and organized with the **Tasks** section, designed to keep you on top of everything.
